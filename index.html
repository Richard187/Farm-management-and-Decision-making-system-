About AgriSmart Pro

Rooted in Zambia, designed for Africa’s smart agricultural transformation

Who We Are

AgriSmart Pro is a smart farm management platform built around the realities of Zambian and African farmers. We translate evidence from behavioural research, agricultural policy, and frontline extension work into actionable digital tools that amplify productivity while safeguarding livelihoods against climate shocks.

How We Help Farmers

We accelerate the adoption of proven smart farming practices by combining localized data, social insights, and farmer-friendly design. Here is how AgriSmart Pro supports resilient decision-making:

Smart Crop Planning

Create adaptive crop calendars that incorporate drought-tolerant varieties, crop rotation, and mulching to maintain soil health and safeguard yields.

Intelligent Water Stewardship

Align irrigation with rainfall forecasts, soil moisture, and watershed health to make every litre count during droughts and flood recovery.

Livestock Resilience

Monitor herd health, plan fodder reserves, and adapt housing to withstand heat stress events while tracking productivity targets.

Climate Intelligence

Combine day-to-day forecasts with long-term climate indicators to prepare for drought, heat waves, and pest outbreaks before they strike.

Climate Finance Readiness

Track investments tied to smart farming upgrades, connect with grants or microfinance, and measure returns on resilient practices.

AI & Behavioural Insights

Receive guidance powered by AI that mirrors trusted advisor networks and accounts for gender, experience, and attitudes toward risk.

Decision-Making Support

Our research-led decision engine brings the human side of adoption into the platform:

  • Behaviour-Informed Analytics: Track how gender, education, and farm size influence adoption journeys to prioritise the right support.
  • Predictive Climate Insights: Forecast yields, rainfall shocks, and market movements to plan proactive responses.
  • Trusted Advisory Pathways: Surface recommendations from extension services, agro-dealers, and peer leaders based on farmer preferences.
  • Resource Optimization: Calibrate inputs such as water, fertilizer, and labour to match smart farming best practices.
  • Risk Management: Receive alerts on impending climate shocks with step-by-step mitigation actions.
  • Climate Finance Guidance: Access blended finance opportunities and evaluate ROI for conservation agriculture investments.

Our Mission

To close the gap between proven smart farming solutions and the farmers who need them. We empower every farmer—from 1-hectare households to commercial estates—with the tools, insights, and networks required to thrive in a changing climate and strengthen food security across Africa.
